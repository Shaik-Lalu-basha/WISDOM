Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

25 years of growth, achievements, and unwavering commitment to education.

2000

Foundation Year

Wisdom High School established by Sri. G. Venkateshwara Rao with 120 students and 8 classrooms in Brahmanakotkur.

2003

First Board Results

First Class 10 batch achieves 100% pass rate — setting the standard for academic excellence.

2008

Infrastructure Expansion

New academic block added with 20 classrooms, Science Labs, Computer Lab, and a Library.

2012

Sports Complex

State-of-the-art sports arena inaugurated. School teams win first District Championship in Cricket.

2016

Digital Revolution

Smart classrooms with interactive boards installed across all classes. Digital Learning Portal launched.

2020

20 Years & 1500 Students

Platinum jubilee celebrations. Robotics Lab, AI Lab, and Language Lab inaugurated. 1500+ students enrolled.

2024

Award & Recognition

Recognized as Best School in Kurnool District. Parent Portal and Student Management System launched.

2026

2000+ Students & Growing

New digital infrastructure, IIT/NEET foundation program, and online admission portal launched for 2026–27.
