How to Apply

Admission Process

Simple and transparent admission process. We are here to help you at every step.

1

Enquiry & Visit

Call us at 8184873311 or visit the school campus to speak with our admissions team. Schedule a campus tour to see our facilities firsthand.

📞 Call Anytime📍 Walk In Welcome
2

Fill Application Form

Collect the admission application form from the school office or fill the online form below. Complete all required details accurately.

📋 Free Application🌐 Online Available
3

Submit Documents

Submit required documents — previous class marksheet, Transfer Certificate (if applicable), Birth Certificate, Aadhaar Card, and passport photos.

📄 Documents🆔 Aadhaar Required
4

Interaction & Assessment

A friendly interaction session with the student and parent to understand the child's strengths, interests, and learning needs.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Meeting📝 Friendly Assessment
5

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Pay the admission fee and receive your child's admission confirmation letter, class allotment, and school schedule.

💳 Easy Payment✅ Seat Confirmed
6

Welcome to Wisdom Family!

Your child joins the Wisdom High School family! Collect books, uniforms, and ID card. School begins on the scheduled date.

🎒 Collect Kit🎉 Welcome Day
