Directions

How to Reach Wisdom High School

Located at Brahmanakotkur Village, Nandikotkur Mandal, Nandyal District — easily accessible from surrounding areas.

🚌 By Bus

1

From Nandyal — Take a bus towards Nandikotkur. Get down at Brahmanakotkur bus stop. School is a short walk from the main road.

2

From Nandikotkur — Regular buses to Brahmanakotkur every 30 minutes from Nandikotkur bus stand.

🚌

School Bus: Our own bus service covers all nearby villages. Call 8184873311 to check your village route.

🚗 By Road

A

From Nandyal — Take SH-516 towards Nandikotkur (~25 km). Turn towards Brahmanakotkur village.

B

From Kurnool — Via NH-40 to Nandyal (~55 km), then SH-516 towards Nandikotkur / Brahmanakotkur.

Search "Wisdom High School Brahmanakotkur" on Google Maps for live navigation.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from parents. Still have doubts? Call us at 8184873311.

What classes are available at Wisdom High School? ▾

We offer education from Baby Class to 10th Class — including Baby Class, LKG, UKG, and Classes 1 through 10. Both Telugu Medium and English Medium are available. The school is Recognised by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Is there bus facility from my village? ▾

Yes! Wisdom High School provides bus service to every village in the surrounding area — ప్రతి గ్రామానికి బస్సు సౌకర్యం కలదు. Call us at 8184873311 to check if your village is covered and for route details.

From which class does IIT/NEET Foundation start? ▾

IIT/NEET Foundation classes are conducted from Class 6 onwards. Our alumni have secured free seats in Medicine, IIT, JEE Mains, and Engineering colleges. Special coaching continues through Class 9 and 10 with dedicated subject teachers.

What are the school timings? ▾

School hours are 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. The school office is open from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. You can visit or call anytime during these hours for enquiries.

Do you provide Computer Education? ▾

Yes, Computer Education is provided to all students — boys and girls — from early classes. We have a dedicated Computer Lab with modern systems. Audio-Video teaching using Projectors and TVs is available in every classroom.

Are Quran classes available for Muslim students? ▾

Yes. Special Quran teaching sessions are conducted in the evening hours for Muslim boys and girls who wish to learn. This is conducted alongside the regular school curriculum.

How do I apply for admission? ▾

Simply call 8184873311 or visit the school. You can also fill the online admission form on our Admissions page. Admissions are open for 2026-27 and seats are limited, so apply early!

Is there special attention for weak students? ▾

Absolutely. Special remedial and improvement classes are conducted by teachers for students who need extra help. Every student gets individual attention, and weak students are given special motivation and a personalised learning plan — చదువులో వెనుకబడిన విద్యార్థులపై ప్రత్యేక శ్రద్ధ.
